Overview

MathCross ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile game similar to crosswords but focused on math. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how the app handles information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store personal information (such as name, email, phone number, precise location, or payment details). MathCross does not require an account and does not maintain user profiles.

The app integrates third-party services that may automatically collect limited information for analytics and advertising purposes, such as device information, app interactions, approximate location, and advertising identifiers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party providers. Their data practices are governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob (advertising). Learn more in Google's Privacy Policy and manage ad personalization in your device settings or via Ads Settings.
  • Google Analytics for Firebase (standard app analytics). See Firebase Privacy & Security.

These providers may receive information such as your device type and OS, IP address, app version, usage events, and advertising identifiers (e.g., AAID on Android or IDFA on iOS), which help deliver ads and understand app performance.

Children’s Privacy

MathCross is suitable for all ages and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected personal information from a child, please contact us at the email below and we will promptly delete it.

Data Storage & Security

We do not operate our own user database. Any information collected through the services above is processed and stored by those providers. We implement reasonable safeguards within the app, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Choices
